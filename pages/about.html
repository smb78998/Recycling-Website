Our Mission


We were founded in 2025 in response to a lack of recycling pick ups within our local communities. Our goal is to provide our users with easily accessible information on what may be recycled, where it may be recycled, and the impact it may have on our environment long term. We seek to make this as easy as possible from an individual level, offering a wealth of information accessible by simply scanning the standard bar code already available on most products.
